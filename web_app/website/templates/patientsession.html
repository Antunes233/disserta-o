The session consists in a normal gait analysis while the patient walks at a normal velocity during a certain period of time.


Once the session ends the data is sent to the database and is processed.


To start the session please press the "Begin Session" button located in the top right side of the page. To end the session please press the button "End session" located next to the "Begin session" button. Before returning to the patient page please press the "Check Progress" button to check if the data was received from the knee brace. If the data is still being processed a message saying "Waiting for data..." will be displayed whe the button "Check Progress" is pressed, otherwise the message "Session Completed" will be displayed.


Steps to begin the session

  1. Stretch the knee brace to its maximum and turn on the device
  2. Wait for the device connects successfully to the WiFi (green led will stop blinking)
  3. Ask the patient to dress the knee braces and walk for a short period of time so they get used to it
  4. When ready, press the "Begin Session" button
